Privacy Policy

Effective date: December 1, 2025

1. What data we collect

  • Registration: email, name, hashed password (bcrypt).
  • Technical: IP address, user-agent, login times — for security and anti-fraud.
  • Payment: amount, method, transaction status. We do not store card details.
  • Content: links to your apps, keywords, and position metrics.

2. Why we use it

  • Providing and improving the Service;
  • Account security (rate limiting, suspicious-activity detection);
  • Transactional notifications (confirmations, receipts) — mandatory;
  • Support — responding to your requests.

3. Sharing with third parties

We use the following processors:

  • Resend — email delivery;
  • Hosting provider — server infrastructure;
  • Telegram — administrator notifications (aggregated event data only, never your password).

We do not sell your data to third parties and do not use it for advertising.

4. Retention and deletion

Data is kept while your account is active. After account deletion, data is removed within 30 days, except data we are legally required to keep (e.g. accounting records — 5 years).

5. Your rights

  • Request a copy of your data;
  • Request correction or deletion;
  • Withdraw consent at any time (this will stop the Service working for you).

Requests: @MayaPush_bot on Telegram.

6. Cookies and localStorage

We store the JWT auth token in your browser's localStorage. There are no third-party trackers or analytics.

7. Security

Passwords are stored as bcrypt hashes. TLS on all connections. A security audit log records logins, password-reset attempts, and balance changes.

8. Changes

We give 14 days' email notice of material changes.
