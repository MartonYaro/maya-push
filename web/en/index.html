/05 · FAQ

Questions &
answers.

How does MAYA Push work?
We pick the keywords you want to rank for and drive motivated installs on them from real devices. The App Store and Google Play see a spike of searches and downloads for the query and lift your app in the results — while you track positions in the dashboard.
What are keyword (motivated) installs?
Downloads made after searching the App Store or Google Play for your app by a specific keyword — not via a direct link. Search + install by query is the key ranking signal that grows your positions.
Do you work with both the App Store and Google Play?
Yes — full ASO promotion and motivated installs for both iOS (App Store) and Android (Google Play). Add a link to either store, set keywords and geo, and we lift your positions on each platform.
How many installs to reach the top?
It depends on the keyword frequency and competition in the geo. Low-frequency queries can reach the top on 50–200 installs, high-frequency ones on a few thousand. You can start with a test and scale up.
When are results visible?
First position moves are usually visible within a few days, with consolidation over 1–2 weeks. After that, organic traffic takes over.
Is it safe for my app?
We use real iPhones and Android devices with local IPs in the target geos — no emulators or proxy farms — at a smooth, human-like pace. That reduces risk compared with bot traffic.
Is this ASO or fake install bots?
This is ASO done right, not bot fraud. Instead of fake installs, we drive real search-and-install sessions on live devices on the keywords you choose — the legitimate ranking signal App Store and Google Play actually reward.
Which geos are available?
12+ countries, including the US, Europe and major markets. You set the geo when configuring a keyword.
How much does it cost? Is there a minimum?
From $0.30 per install, dropping with volume. No minimum deposit — start with any amount; funds are fully spent on installs.
How do I top up the balance?
Via a manager (they send the details in Telegram) or with crypto. The balance is credited after payment and then spent on installs.
How are positions tracked?
We parse current positions straight from the App Store and Google Play and refresh them several times a day. The dashboard shows the trend for each keyword and geo.
Still have questions?
Write to our support bot — it goes straight to a manager, the reply comes back in the same chat.
